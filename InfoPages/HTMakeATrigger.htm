How to Make a Trigger

Posted by Retera

From Summons of the Void & Worlds of Infinity

  • For those who don't know, Warcraft triggers are a programming language on top of the actual one that runs Warcraft III (WC3). Triggers were developed by Blizzard Entertainment (Creator of WC3 and the WC3 Editor) in order to make map programming easier for mapmakers.
  • There is another language of WC3 called "Scripts", which is harder to use (in my opinion, which is based on the fact that I learned triggers first).

 

  • Now, on to how to use/make triggers!
  • Triggers consist of Actions, Conditions, and Events.
  • Events are what must happen to activate a trigger. Right click on the word events and hit "Create New Event"
